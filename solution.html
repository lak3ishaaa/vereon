🌟 Solution: Vereon

To solve the problem of impersonation and unauthorized access during exams, VEREON uses smart facial recognition technology 👁️‍🗨️ to create a reliable and easy-to-use access control system 🔐.

This system helps exam invigilators 🧑‍🏫 quickly and accurately confirm the identity of each registered candidate before they take the test 📝. By doing this, VEREON ensures that only the right people are allowed to sit for the exams, reducing cheating and making the process more secure ✅.

Traditional methods like checking ID cards 🪪 can be slow and are sometimes not enough to catch impersonators. VEREON improves this by using facial recognition to verify each person in real time ⏱️. This saves time, reduces mistakes ❌, and increases trust in the exam process 🤝.
